There was a strange English classroom in the school I used to go to.
When I walked into the room, I always felt someone tap me on my shoulder.

Even if I was the only one there.

Every day, the wallpaper started off white, but by the time I left, it turned a creepy blood red.

And that's not all.

Whenever I looked at the clock, I saw eyes staring at me from seemingly inside its surface.

The eyes were terrifying, black as coal and glaring directly at me.

But no one else seemed to notice.

That classroom was the reason for the first time I ever ditched class.

I skipped English countless times that year just to avoid the classroom.

At least now, those are just distant memories that I don't have to worry about.
